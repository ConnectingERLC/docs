<Accordion title="Supervisory" defaultOpen icon="headset">
  The**Supervisor**  rank is the lowest rank in the **High Rank** section. Their Task is to keep lower staff in line and moderate self promotion and slow-mode channels.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Management" defaultOpen icon="newspaper">
  The**Management**  team is the second lowest rank in the**High Rank**  section. This is split into Management, Senior Management, and Lead Management. They all have the same tasks to as Supervisor, but they manage the supervisor role, and they get access to tickets.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Directive" defaultOpen icon="crown">
  The**Directive**  team is the highest rank in the**High Rank**  section. This is slip into Assistant Director, Deputy Director, and Director. Their tasks are to manage**ALL**  ranks. They have access to**ALL**  tickets and have infraction and promotion permission.
</Accordion>
