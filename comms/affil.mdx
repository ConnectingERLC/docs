## Requirements

Your server must meet the following requirements to get an affiliation.

<Info>
  We still offer partnerships with less requirements if you don't meet these.
</Info>

- 500 member minimum
- Active Community
- 10 staff
- Must make a channel named "connecting-erlc" with our server link.

  **Open a ticket to apply.**

  <Warning>
    We will evaluate your server. Prepare to give us permissions to view all channels.
  </Warning>
